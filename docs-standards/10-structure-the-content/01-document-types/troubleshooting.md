# Troubleshooting - Help me to resolve issues

> ℹ️ **Note:**
>
> For the template associated to this topic, see [troubleshooting template]().

A troubleshooting topic or section explains to users the product known issues and errors they might encounter because of product quirks or users' mistakes, and helps users resolve issues effectively if there is a solution.

When an issue applies to a specific procedure article, document it in that article as a troubleshooting section. Otherwise, create a new troubleshooting topic.

### Subtitle

One short, imperative sentence that describes the issue and optionally the cause of the issue.

Guidelines:

- Single sentence with no links, list items, or formatting. Ends with a period.
- Use H4 format. Stay under 120 characters / 20 words.
- Adds new value beyond the title. It should not repeat the title or be a rephrased version of it.

> ⚙️ **Example:**
> 
> - As a security precaution, GitHub automatically deletes SSH keys that haven't been used in a year.
> - This error means your CA root certificate is out of date.
